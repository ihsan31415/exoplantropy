About Our Mission

Our mission at ExoPlanTropy is to democratize the hunt for exoplanets. We provide accessible tools for enthusiasts and researchers alike to analyze data from space telescopes and uncover new worlds hidden among the stars.

Our mission is to accelerate the discovery of new worlds and deepen our understanding of the cosmos. By automating the initial analysis, we empower astronomers to focus on the most promising candidates, bringing us one step closer to answering humanity's oldest question: Are we alone?

The Team
