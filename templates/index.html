Our mission

A concise roadmap that explains who it serves and how it delivers impact

We aim to provide a comprehensive and user-friendly platform for all users.

People we had in mind

Researchers, students, and self-learners eager to understand how astronomical data is transformed into new planet discoveries.

Core experience

A web platform that hosts trained ML models and visual tools to analyze NASA data and discover exoplanet candidates.

Ideal moments

Learning sessions, research experiments, or when evaluating new data from the combined Kepler, K2, and TESS missions.

Where it lives

Everything runs in your browser—no heavy installations.

Impact

Proper feature engineering enhances model accuracy and helps the scientific community focus on promising planetary signals.
