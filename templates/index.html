What do these numbers mean?

  • Median helps you choose realistic starting values when a feature isn’t available.
  • Mean shows the overall trend, perfect for spotting how extreme your input is.
  • Std (standard deviation) tells you how much samples vary around the average.

Use all three together for basic feature engineering—for example, normalizing new data before uploading it.

AI assistant

Entropy, your narrative explainer

Entropy uses Google Gemini to turn data tables into accessible scientific narratives. Activate it by adding GEMINI_API_KEY to your environment.

  • ◆ Ask “Why is this candidate promising?” and get a narrated response.
  • ◆ Summarize the top batch results for your team report.
  • ◆ Use the copy-context button if you want to move the raw JSON into another tool.
